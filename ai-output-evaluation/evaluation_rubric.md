# AI Output Evaluation Rubric

## Purpose
This rubric is designed to provide a structured approach for evaluating AI-generated outputs. It helps ensure that the outputs meet the necessary standards of quality, relevance, and coherence.

## Evaluation Criteria

1. **Accuracy**
   - Definition: The degree to which the AI output correctly represents the intended information or task.
   - Scale: 
     - 1: Completely inaccurate
     - 2: Mostly inaccurate
     - 3: Somewhat accurate
     - 4: Mostly accurate
     - 5: Completely accurate

2. **Relevance**
   - Definition: The extent to which the output is pertinent to the input or the context in which it is generated.
   - Scale:
     - 1: Not relevant at all
     - 2: Slightly relevant
     - 3: Moderately relevant
     - 4: Very relevant
     - 5: Highly relevant

3. **Coherence**
   - Definition: The logical flow and clarity of the output, including how well the ideas are connected.
   - Scale:
     - 1: Completely incoherent
     - 2: Mostly incoherent
     - 3: Somewhat coherent
     - 4: Mostly coherent
     - 5: Completely coherent

4. **Creativity**
   - Definition: The originality and inventiveness of the output, particularly in tasks requiring creative thinking.
   - Scale:
     - 1: Not creative at all
     - 2: Slightly creative
     - 3: Moderately creative
     - 4: Very creative
     - 5: Highly creative

5. **Engagement**
   - Definition: The ability of the output to capture and maintain the interest of the intended audience.
   - Scale:
     - 1: Not engaging at all
     - 2: Slightly engaging
     - 3: Moderately engaging
     - 4: Very engaging
     - 5: Highly engaging

## Usage
Evaluators should use this rubric to assess AI-generated outputs systematically.
